Background

Festival Doodles are fun, surprising and sometimes spontaneous changes that are made to the Search Engine logo to celebrate holidays, anniversaries and the lives of celebrities. After the concept of the doodle was born by Google founders in the 1998, the demand for doodles has risen in internationally. Creating doodles is now implemented by many search engines, and SOSO is one of them.

Program Flow

SOSO Logo

The basic SOSO loge includes three parts—English product name, Chinese product name, and slogan. To make doodles more creative, the core concept of SOSO festival doodle design is to reflect the shape of the four English letters--“SOSO” with items which are relevant to a special theme. Since the shape of Chinese is too difficult to fit in an illustration, festival doodles do not include the Chinese name and the slogan.

Guidance

As the SOSO festival doodles were showed both on traditional web page and mobile web, I regulated the size of doodle on the two occasions to ensure works of different designers being practical.
